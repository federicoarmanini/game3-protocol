Mission

About

NodeStrike was born from a simple idea: GameFi should be accessible to everyone, not just those who can afford to invest upfront.

🎯 Our Vision

We believe the future of gaming lies at the intersection of entertainment and ownership. Every player deserves the right to earn real value from their time and skill — without paywalls, without gatekeeping.

⛓ Built on Polygon

NodeStrike runs entirely on Polygon Mainnet — fast, low-cost, and environmentally friendly. Every $NODE token is minted on-chain, every transaction is transparent and verifiable by anyone.

🚀 Where We're Going

NodeStrike is in active development. Smart contracts are live and verified on Polygon. The game is playable today. What comes next — staking, NFT marketplace, DAO governance — is being built in the open, one commit at a time.
